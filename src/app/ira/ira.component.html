Traditional IRA

A savings account that allows your contributions to be deducted from your taxable income. Then when you retire with your IRA, your money will be taxed at your ordinary-income tax rate.

Annual Contribution Limits:

  • $6,000/year for ages under 50
  • $7,000/year for ages 50 and older
